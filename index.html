Beavers is where the adventure begins! Exploring the outdoors, making crafts, playing games, and making friends.

When: Mondays 5:30–7:00pm
Leader: Mrs. Sarah Thornton
Where: Terrington Village Hall

🦫

Terrington Beaver Colony

Motto: "Fun, friendship, and sharing!"

🐯

Terrington Cub Pack

Motto: "Do Your Best"

Ages 8–10½

🐯 Cubs

First aid, cooking, camping, sports badges. All about teamwork and having a great time.

When: Wednesdays 6:00–7:30pm
Leader: Mr. James Whitfield
Where: Terrington Village Hall

Ages 10½–14

⚜️ Scouts

Camping, navigation, pioneering, cooking on open fires. Duke of Edinburgh Award available.

When: Thursdays 6:30–8:30pm
Leader: Mr. David Hargreaves
Where: Scout HQ, Church Lane

Why Join Scouts?

Scouting gives young people skills for life — confidence, resilience, teamwork, and a love of the outdoors. We've been doing this in Terrington since 1923.

🏕️

Regular camping trips and outdoor adventures across North Yorkshire

🏅

Over 200 activity badges including Duke of Edinburgh

👫

A welcoming, inclusive community with lifelong friendships

🌍

Opportunities to volunteer and serve your local community
