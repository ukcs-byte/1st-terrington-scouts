Our Sections

Meet Our Sections

Four sections, one family — each with its own character and adventures

Ages 5½–8

🦫 Beavers

Beavers is where the adventure begins! Our youngest members explore the outdoors, make crafts, play games, and make friends in a welcoming colony setting. Every session is a new discovery.

When: Mondays 5:30pm – 7:00pm
Leader: Akela (Mrs. Sarah Thornton)
Meeting Place: Terrington Village Hall

🦫

Terrington Beaver Colony

Motto: "Beaver Scout bids our motto be, Fun, friendship, and sharing!"

🐯

Terrington Cub Pack

Motto: "Do Your Best"

Ages 8–10½

🐯 Cubs

Cubs take on new challenges every week — from first aid and cooking, to camping trips and sports badges. It's all about teamwork, perseverance and having a great time.

When: Wednesdays 6:00pm – 7:30pm
Leader: Baloo (Mr. James Whitfield)
Meeting Place: Terrington Village Hall

Ages 10½–14

⚜️ Scouts

Real adventures await in Scouts. Camping, navigation, pioneering, cooking on open fires — our Troop gets out into the wild and challenges themselves every term. Duke of Edinburgh Award available.

When: Thursdays 6:30pm – 8:30pm
Leader: Skip (Mr. David Hargreaves)
Meeting Place: Scout HQ, Church Lane

⚜️

Terrington Scout Troop

Motto: "Be Prepared"

🧭

Terrington Explorer Unit

Motto: "Challenge Yourself"

Ages 14–18

🧭 Explorers

Explorers take the lead. International expeditions, community service projects, Gold Duke of Edinburgh, and leadership training. The senior section where our members truly come into their own.

When: Fridays 7:00pm – 9:30pm
Leader: ESL (Ms. Rachel Booth)
Meeting Place: Scout HQ, Church Lane

Why Join Scouts?

Scouting gives young people skills for life — confidence, resilience, teamwork, and a love of the outdoors. We've been doing this in Terrington since 1923.

🏕️

Regular camping trips and outdoor adventures across North Yorkshire and beyond

🏅

Over 200 activity badges to earn across all sections, including Duke of Edinburgh

👫

A welcoming, inclusive community with lifelong friendships

🌍

Opportunities to volunteer and serve your local community

💰

Subsidised subscription fees — no young person turned away for financial reasons

Questions before you join?

Call Sarah on 01653 648200 or email join@1stterringtonscouts.org.uk
